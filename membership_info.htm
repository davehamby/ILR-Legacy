Becoming a Member of the Institute
How to Join

You can join Old Dominion University's Institute for Learning in Retirement by submitting a Membership Registration Form. 

Memberships are available as follows:

  • June 1 through May 31 -- annual fee of $15 per person.

To obtain a Membership Registration Form you may download one from http://www.oduilr.com/membership.pdf or by calling the office at (757) 368-4160. You will be put on the mailing list for the next term. Catalogs are mailed about three weeks before the registration deadline for the term.

To obtain the current schedule of courses, you may download one from http://www.oduilr.com/sched.htm or by calling the office at (757) 368-4160.

Classes and other events are offered during fall, winter, spring, and summer terms.
